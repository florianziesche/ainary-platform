## Investment Stage Definitions

### Pre-Seed ($250K - $1M)
- **Company state:** Idea, prototype, pre-revenue
- **Investors:** Angels, pre-seed funds, accelerators
- **NYC examples:** ERA, Notation Capital, Charge Ventures
- **Valuation range:** $2-6M pre-money

### Seed ($1-4M)
- **Company state:** MVP, early customers, seeking PMF
- **Investors:** Seed funds, early-stage VCs
- **NYC examples:** Lerer Hippeau, Bowery Capital, Work-Bench
- **Valuation range:** $6-15M pre-money

### Series A ($5-15M)
- **Company state:** Product-market fit, scaling GTM
- **Investors:** Traditional VCs
- **NYC examples:** FirstMark, RRE, USV
- **Valuation range:** $20-50M pre-money

### Series B ($15-50M)
- **Company state:** Scaling proven model
- **Investors:** Growth-oriented VCs
- **NYC examples:** Insight Partners, General Catalyst
- **Valuation range:** $50-150M pre-money

### Series C+ / Growth ($50M+)
- **Company state:** Market leader, path to exit
- **Investors:** Growth equity, crossover funds
- **NYC examples:** Tiger Global, Coatue, Altimeter
- **Valuation range:** $150M+
