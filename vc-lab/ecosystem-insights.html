Key Insights for Ainary Ventures

1. Ainary's peer group is Conviction, Notation, Contrary. All are thesis-driven emerging managers with technical founder-GPs. Conviction's trajectory (Fund I $101M → Fund II in 2 years) shows the path when thesis + timing align.
2. The AI/Enterprise cluster is where Ainary fits. Work-Bench (enterprise seed) + Conviction (AI infra) + Lux (deep tech) define the neighborhood. Ainary's "vertical AI for B2B" thesis sits at their intersection — differentiated because nobody owns the operator-investor angle in manufacturing + legal AI.
3. Co-investment opportunities are clear. Lerer Hippeau, Primary, and Bowery frequently co-invest at seed. Ainary should build relationships now for deal sharing. FirstMark and Lux are natural Series A leads for Ainary's portfolio companies.
4. The LP path is well-defined. Cendana (Michael Kim) is the gold standard for emerging seed managers. Thema VC is purpose-built for Fund I GPs. Both have clear application processes. The NYC Pension Funds have explicit emerging + diverse manager mandates — relevant for Fund II+.
5. European bridge = unique deal flow. No firm on this list systematically sources European AI companies entering the US market. This is Ainary's structural advantage — German engineering + NYC market access creates a deal flow channel that doesn't exist elsewhere.
