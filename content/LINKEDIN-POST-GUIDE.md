## ðŸ—£ï¸ Language & Tone

### DO:
- Write like you talk
- Use "you" and "I" (personal)
- Active voice, present tense
- Specific > vague ("15 hours/week" not "a lot of time")
- Confident but not arrogant

### DON'T:
- Corporate speak ("leverage synergies")
- Excessive "I" (make it about the reader/solution)
- Humble bragging
- Asking for engagement ("Like if you agree!")
- Generic advice without proof
