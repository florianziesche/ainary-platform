## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### Mistake #1: Posting Too Much Too Early
**Problem:** At <500 followers, posting 10x/day = shouting into void  
**Solution:** 1 quality post + 50 quality engagements > 5 mediocre posts  
**Why:** Your posts won't reach many people; others' posts will

### Mistake #2: Only Talking About Yourself
**Problem:** "I did this, I built that, hire me, buy my thing"  
**Solution:** 80% value/education, 20% self-promotion  
**Why:** People follow value, not sales pitches

### Mistake #3: No Visual Differentiation
**Problem:** Text-only tweets in a visual world  
**Solution:** Add screenshots, simple graphics, formatting  
**Why:** Visual posts get 2-3x more engagement

### Mistake #4: Inconsistent Presence
**Problem:** Post for 3 weeks, disappear for 2, repeat  
**Solution:** Show up daily, even if just to engage  
**Why:** Algorithm rewards consistency; audience forgets you

### Mistake #5: Chasing Viral Instead of Building
**Problem:** Optimizing for retweets over follower quality  
**Solution:** Build genuine audience that cares about your niche  
**Why:** 1,000 engaged followers > 10,000 random ones

### Mistake #6: Ignoring Your Replies
**Problem:** Post and ghost  
**Solution:** Respond to every reply in first 2 hours  
**Why:** Engagement signals to algorithm; builds community

### Mistake #7: Being Generic
**Problem:** "AI is changing everything" "Build in public" "Just shipped"  
**Solution:** Specific, personal, contrarian when appropriate  
**Why:** Generic = forgettable; specific = shareable

### Mistake #8: No Clear POV
**Problem:** Trying to appeal to everyone  
**Solution:** Strong opinions, weakly held; pick a lane  
**Why:** Strong POV attracts right people, repels wrong people (this is good)

### Mistake #9: Copying Others' Style
**Problem:** Being "Pieter Levels lite" or "fake Jack Butcher"  
**Solution:** Learn from them, but find YOUR voice  
**Why:** Audience wants authentic you, not poor imitation

### Mistake #10: Giving Up Too Early
**Problem:** Quitting after 30 days because "it's not working"  
**Solution:** Commit to 90 days minimum before evaluating  
**Why:** Compound effects take time; Month 3 â‰  Month 1
