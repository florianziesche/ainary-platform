## Playbooks

### AB-trustcalibration-PLAY-0001
- **Trigger:** Initiating adaptive trust calibration in AI systems.
- **Goal:** Implement adaptive trust calibration methods effectively.
- **Inputs:** AI system architecture, user feedback data.
- **Steps:**
  1. Assess current trust calibration methods.
  2. Collect and analyze user feedback.
  3. Implement adaptive methods based on feedback.
  4. Monitor and adjust trust levels continuously.
- **Outputs:** Enhanced trust calibration strategy.
- **Failure modes:** Inadequate user feedback, system resistance to adaptation.
- **Mitigations:** Establish robust feedback mechanisms, ensure system flexibility.
- **Acceptance criteria:** Improved user trust and collaboration metrics.

### AB-trustcalibration-PLAY-0002
- **Trigger:** Continuous evaluation of trust levels in AI systems.
- **Goal:** Maintain appropriate trust levels through ongoing evaluation.
- **Inputs:** User feedback, trust metrics.
- **Steps:**
  1. Set up continuous feedback loops.
  2. Regularly review trust metrics.
  3. Adjust trust calibration strategies as needed.
- **Outputs:** Consistent trust levels aligned with system capabilities.
- **Failure modes:** Feedback fatigue, outdated metrics.
- **Mitigations:** Rotate feedback sources, update metrics regularly.
- **Acceptance criteria:** Stable trust levels over time.

### AB-trustcalibration-PLAY-0003
- **Trigger:** Identifying risks due to improper trust calibration.
- **Goal:** Mitigate risks associated with trust calibration failures.
- **Inputs:** Risk assessment reports, trust calibration data.
- **Steps:**
  1. Conduct a risk assessment of current trust calibration.
  2. Identify potential failure points.
  3. Implement mitigation strategies.
- **Outputs:** Reduced risk of over-reliance or under-utilization of AI.
- **Failure modes:** Unidentified risks, ineffective mitigations.
- **Mitigations:** Regular risk reviews, adaptive mitigation plans.
- **Acceptance criteria:** Decreased incidence of trust-related failures.
