36ZERO Vision · Strategic Report 01

The Customer Problem

Why visual quality control is failing today — and what it costs.

Manual inspection is the foundation of quality control in manufacturing. And it's crumbling. The numbers are alarming:

80-85% Human Inspector Detection Rate
up to 40% False Positive Rate (rule-based)
15-20% Cost of Poor Quality (% Revenue)

The Five Biggest Pain Points

Fatigue & Inconsistency

Even experienced inspectors max out at 80-85% detection. Attention drops after 2 hours. Worse in the evening than morning. Different on Monday vs. Friday. Source: Pharmaceutical Technology

Knowledge Drain

13-20 million workers retiring in Germany by 2036. 30 years of experience — "I know this crack, it's the spindle" — disappearing permanently. A global phenomenon.

Scrap & Rework

Cost of Poor Quality: 8-15% of revenue in automotive, aerospace, medtech. ASQ estimates 15-20% for typical manufacturers. Source: ASQ, bprhub.com

Recall Costs

28 million vehicles recalled in the US alone in 2024 (445 NHTSA campaigns). A single recall: $97-194M (GM 2024). Source: NHTSA, CBS News

How 36ZERO Solves These Problems

24/7 Consistency

AI doesn't get tired. Same detection rate at 3 AM as at 9 AM. Every part, every shift, around the clock.

Digitize Knowledge

Expert labels 20 images → 30 years of experience stored as an AI model. The knowledge stays, even when the person leaves.

Early Detection

Catch defects at the start of the process, not the end. Defect cost increases 10x per production stage.

Data-Efficient

5-20 images instead of thousands. Perfect for rare defects, new products, and regulated industries with limited defect data.

36ZERO Vision · Strategic Report 03

Agentic Quality

The next frontier: autonomous agents that don't just find defects — they fix them.

Agentic AI is the hottest trend in industrial software. The core idea: AI agents that don't just detect — they act autonomously, without human intervention in the loop.

The Concrete Workflow

01

Detect

Camera identifies a crack on a brake disc. This is what 36ZERO does today — fast, reliable, with just 5 training images.

02

Diagnose

Agent correlates with process parameters and knowledge base: spindle temperature 12°C above threshold since 14:32. Tool wear at 87%. Similar issue at 3 other customers — root cause was always calibration.

03

Prescribe

Agent recommends: spindle recalibration, tool change after current batch, material testing of the last shipment.

04

Act

Agent sends correction command to PLC/MES via Bosch ctrlX, pauses production if critical, auto-documents in ERP.

Evidence: Closed-Loop Already Works

In injection molding, closed-loop quality control already exists: AI detects process drift, identifies causes, and automatically adjusts machine parameters — pressure, temperature, injection speed, cooling time.

-23% Defects (Automotive Supplier)
€89K Avoided Failure Costs
24/7 Autonomous Operation

36ZERO's infrastructure advantage: SAP partnership provides ERP data (diagnosis). Bosch ctrlX provides PLC connectivity (action). The building blocks for Autonomous Quality already exist.

Self-Improving AI

Already reality for training today — and the key to the Autonomous Quality loop.

36ZERO already uses Self-Improving principles today: data-efficient training with 5-20 images, active learning from user feedback. Next step: full automation — a system that continuously improves itself.

Three Dimensions of Self-Improvement

01

Self-Training: AI Learns from Every Part

Every inspected part is a training data point. Active Learning: the system asks for labels on edge cases. Detection accuracy improves with every shift.

02

Self-Calibrating: AI Controls the Environment

Actively controls lighting (brightness, angle, wavelength). Cameras auto-calibrate for vibrations or temperature changes.

03

Self-Adapting: New Product? No Problem.

Product changeovers require weeks with traditional systems. Self-Improving AI recognizes the new baseline and starts detecting defects immediately.

0 Engineers for Retraining
1 Click User Confirms Suggestion
99% Defect Reduction (WEF Lighthouses)

The Flywheel Effect: More inspections → more data → better model → fewer errors → more trust → more inspections. Traditional systems degrade. Self-Improving AI gets better over time.
