**Source:** Lenny's Newsletter (How I AI)  
**Link:** https://www.lennysnewsletter.com/p/this-week-on-how-i-ai-5-openclaw  
**Published:** 2026-03-02  
**Relevance:** 10/10 — Multi-agent architecture, progressive trust, security patterns

**Summary:** Jesse Genet runs FIVE specialized OpenClaw agents, each on its own Mac Mini, for homeschooling, finances, scheduling, development, and household operations. - Physical inventory integration: "pull out the tracing board from the cupboard"
