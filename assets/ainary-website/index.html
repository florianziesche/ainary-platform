Perspective

Where AI meets scarce expertise

The $11T global labor market is 24× the $450B software market. The real opportunity isn't chatbots — it's replacing entire workflows in industries where expertise is scarce and labor is expensive.

Vertical AI beats horizontal AI. The moat is domain knowledge encoded into systems, not model performance. The best AI is built by people who've worked in the industries they're automating — not by observers writing about it from the sideline.

I've built from both sides: as a startup CEO shipping computer vision to automotive OEMs, and as an operator scaling a 150-person organization. The pattern is consistent. Operators build better AI. And the bridge between European industrial depth and American market scale is an arbitrage that compounds.
