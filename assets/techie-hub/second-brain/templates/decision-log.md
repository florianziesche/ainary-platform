> [!info] When to Create a Decision Log
> - Decisions with long-term impact (>3 months)
> - Decisions affecting multiple people or projects
> - Strategic choices (product direction, hiring, pricing)
> - Major time or money commitments
> - Anything you might second-guess later

> [!tip] Decision Log Tips
> - Create BEFORE deciding (use it to think through options)
> - Include the people who need to be aligned
> - Set a review date (3-6 months out)
> - Actually review it â€” learn from outcomes
> - Link to project notes where decision gets implemented

> [!warning] Avoiding Decision Debt
> Undocumented decisions = repeated debates.
> 
> When someone asks "Why did we do it this way?" â†’ link to the decision log.
