### Market Context (February 2026)

#### Industry Trends
- ‚ö†Ô∏è **Tighter hiring:** Some funds trimmed teams (Initialized, NFX, Greycroft)
- ‚úÖ **[[AI]] still hot:** [[AI]]-focused funds continue to raise and deploy capital
- ‚úÖ **Operator backgrounds valued:** Market favors those who've built, not just analyzed
- ‚ö†Ô∏è **Competition:** Many founders transitioning to [[VC]]

#### Timing Considerations
- **Good time to look:** Early in year = when funds plan hiring for year ahead
- **Patient approach:** Most funds hire slowly, build relationships over months
- **Network effect:** VC hiring is 80% network, 20% application (leverage [[VC Lab]]!)

#### Salary Expectations (Associate Level, NYC)
- **Seed funds (Betaworks size):** $85K - $125K base + carry
- **Larger funds (General Catalyst):** $100K - $150K base + carry
- **Top-tier (Lux, Greylock):** $120K - $175K base + meaningful carry
- **Note:** Carry becomes meaningful after 4-5 years (long-term game)
