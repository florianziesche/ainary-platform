## The Core Insight

> "The ones who have the systems in place will outperform others by 100x or more over time, with systems and compounding knowledge and memory."

> "Whatever task you do during the day, think about how you can automate [[AI]] to do the task."

## The Vision

Build an interconnected system where every interaction, every piece of knowledge, and every tool compounds over time. 

**This is not about working harder — it's about building infrastructure that works for you 24/7.**

---

## Key Components

### 1. Morning Brief
Automated daily briefing with:
- Calendar overview
- Priority tasks
- Relevant news/insights
- What to focus on today

### 2. Compounded Learning
Every input (article, conversation, meeting) gets:
- Captured in [[Obsidian]]
- Connected to existing knowledge
- Surfaced when relevant

### 3. Content Engine
- Analyze newsletters and news
- Generate insights
- Create distribution-ready content
- [[Content-Engine]]

### 4. Revenue Systems
- Notion Operating System (100x) → [[CEO-Framework-Bundle]]
- Consulting/Freelance pipeline
- Course/educational content

---

## The Compound Loop

```
Input (Reading, Meetings, Research)
    ↓
Capture (Obsidian, Memory)
    ↓
Connect (Wikilinks, Tags)
    ↓
Compound (Patterns, Insights)
    ↓
Output (Content, Decisions, Products)
    ↓
Feedback (Results, Learning)
    ↓
[Repeat — each cycle builds on previous]
```

## North Star Metric

**Time Leverage Ratio:**
Hours of output value ÷ Hours of input work

Goal: 10:1 → 100:1 over time
