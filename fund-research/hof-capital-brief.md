## Investment Thesis: "March to Abundance"

HOF believes technology enables humanity's progress toward abundance. They back founders taking **fundamental technology or business model risk** — not "safe" ventures using proven models.

### Core Thesis Areas:

1. **AI & Automation**
   - Freedom from repetitive work
   - LLMs completing cognitive tasks at human proficiency
   - Both AI infrastructure AND applications
   - "Multi-trillion dollar opportunity"

2. **Financial Infrastructure**
   - Financial services access globally
   - Fintech, crypto/decentralized networks
   - Team has roots in developing economies

3. **Biotech & Genomics**
   - Gene editing (CRISPR)
   - Eliminating genetic disease
   - Long-term: aging, infertility, biological limitations

4. **Human Interconnection**
   - VR/AR, brain-computer interfaces
   - New forms of commerce, entertainment, work
