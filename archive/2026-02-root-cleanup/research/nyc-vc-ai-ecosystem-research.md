## 3. Recent AI Industry Data Points for VC Fund Pitch

### Data Point 1: AI Captured 50% of All Global VC Funding in 2025 ($202B)
- **Source:** Crunchbase, "6 Charts That Show The Big AI Funding Trends Of 2025"
- **Date:** December 15, 2025
- **Data:** AI funding reached **$202.3 billion** in 2025, up **75%** from $114B in 2024. AI now captures **~50% of all global startup funding**, up from 34% in 2024. The U.S. accounted for $159B (79%) of global AI funding.
- **Pitch Use:** Demonstrates that AI is not a passing trend ‚Äî it's *the* asset class. A dedicated AI fund is positioned at the center of where capital is flowing. Half of all VC dollars globally are now going to AI.
- **Link:** [news.crunchbase.com/ai/big-funding-trends-charts-eoy-2025/](https://news.crunchbase.com/ai/big-funding-trends-charts-eoy-2025/)

### Data Point 2: Enterprise AI Revenue Hit $37B in 2025, Up 3x YoY ‚Äî Startups Win 63%
- **Source:** Menlo Ventures, "2025: The State of Generative AI in the Enterprise"
- **Date:** December 9, 2025
- **Data:** Enterprise AI revenue surged from **$1.7B (2023) ‚Üí $37B (2025)** ‚Äî a 22x increase in two years. AI applications now represent **6% of the entire global SaaS market**. Crucially, **startups captured 63% of the AI application market**, earning nearly $2 for every $1 earned by incumbents. Categories like coding (71% startup share), sales (78%), and finance/ops (91%) are being reshaped by startups.
- **Pitch Use:** This is the strongest data point for a startup-focused AI fund thesis. Startups are *winning* against incumbents in enterprise AI ‚Äî this is the moment to invest early. The TAM is growing exponentially and startups have structural advantages.
- **Link:** [menlovc.com/perspective/2025-the-state-of-generative-ai-in-the-enterprise/](https://menlovc.com/perspective/2025-the-state-of-generative-ai-in-the-enterprise/)

### Data Point 3: North American VC Funding Soared 46% to $280B in 2025, AI Driving the Surge
- **Source:** Crunchbase, "North American Startup Funding Soared 46% In 2025, Driven By AI Boom"
- **Date:** January 2026 (published ~3 weeks ago)
- **Data:** Investors poured **$280 billion** into seed-through-growth-stage rounds for U.S. and Canadian startups in 2025, a **46% increase** over 2024. AI was the primary driver. AI's share of VC has grown from ~20% in 2022 to nearly **50% in 2025**. Seed funding records were broken, with AI seed rounds leading the way.
- **Pitch Use:** The overall VC market is recovering and growing *because of* AI. This isn't a zero-sum shift ‚Äî AI is expanding the entire pie. Seed-stage AI is the fastest-growing segment, validating early-stage AI fund strategies.
- **Link:** [news.crunchbase.com/venture/north-american-startup-funding-2025-data-ai-us-investment/](https://news.crunchbase.com/venture/north-american-startup-funding-2025-data-ai-us-investment/)
