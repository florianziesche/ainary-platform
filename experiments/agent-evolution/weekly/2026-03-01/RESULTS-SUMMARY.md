## Winner: THREE-WAY TIE — Groups B, D, F (43/50 each)

### Group B (Inversion) — "Economic Embodiment"
- Strongest failure mapping of any group
- Value Created:Captured ratio (3:1 = intervention) is immediately implementable
- "Revenue Theater" as named anti-pattern

### Group D (Adversarial) — "Deflationary Relationship"
- Most genuinely contrarian: optimize to NEED less, not EARN more
- Variance Farmer concept (capture outliers, don't grind linearly)
- Anti-Sycophancy Clause as explicit protocol element

### Group F (Socratic) — "Financial Amygdala Dampener"
- Deepest psychological insight: AI absorbs financial anxiety so human operates from abundance
- Three-layer model (Survival Floor → Positioning Ceiling → Compounding Zone) cleanest architecture
- "Degrees of freedom" as the true optimization target

## Convergent Themes (Appeared in 7+ groups)

1. **Send-First Lock** — Every single group independently arrived at "block builds until sends happen" (10/10)
2. **Runway > Revenue** — Optimize for survival/freedom, not income maximization (9/10)
3. **AI as Anti-Procrastination Forcing Function** — The agent's core economic role is breaking the overbuild loop (9/10)
4. **Revenue Velocity > Volume** — Time-to-cash matters more than deal size (8/10)
5. **Tiered behavior based on runway** — Different modes for survival/growth/expansion (8/10)

## Divergent Ideas (Unique to 1-2 groups)

1. **Negative satisfaction correlation** (E) — Agent should make human uncomfortable 40-60% of the time; satisfaction is a lagging danger indicator
2. **Calving Ledger** (J) — Track 80%-done work and "release" accumulated value monthly
3. **Planned obsolescence** (C) — Agent should optimize to be replaced by humans (= revenue escaped survival mode)
4. **Deflationary relationship** (D) — Need less > earn more; reduce burn rate as strategic lever
5. **Financial amygdala dampener** (F) — AI carries the anxiety so human operates from psychological abundance
6. **Antifragile pricing** (J) — Price based on client's alternative cost, not provider's time
7. **Economic metabolism** (I) — Track how fast the human can absorb surplus into growth
