## â“ Discovery Questions

### Situation (Current State)
- "Tell me about your current [process/system/approach]?"
- "How long have you been doing it this way?"
- "What tools are you using today?"
- "Who's involved in this process?"

### Problem (Pain Points)
- "What's the biggest challenge you're facing with [X]?"
- "How is this impacting your [team/revenue/goals]?"
- "What have you tried before to solve this?"
- "What happens if you don't solve this?"

### Implication (Cost of Inaction)
- "How much time/money is this costing you?"
- "What opportunities are you missing because of this?"
- "How does this affect your team?"

### Need-Payoff (Vision of Success)
- "What would ideal look like?"
- "If you solved this, what would change?"
- "What's driving the urgency now?"
