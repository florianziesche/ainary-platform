# CNC Fertigungsplaner — Pricing Strategy

**Target: Regional machine shops (Sachsen)**

---

## Pricing Tiers

### Free Demo
- Time calculation
- Basic quote generation
- Single part type
- **Purpose:** Get foot in door

### Pro — €49/month
- Unlimited calculations
- Quote templates
- PDF export
- Email support
- **Target:** 1-5 employee shops

### Business — €149/month
- Everything in Pro
- Multiple users
- Custom branding
- Priority support
- API access
- **Target:** 5-20 employee shops

### Enterprise — Custom
- On-premise option
- ERP integration
- Training
- **Target:** Larger manufacturers
