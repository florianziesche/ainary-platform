# CNC Planner Pro — Go-to-Market Strategy

**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** February 2026

---

## Executive Summary

CNC Planner Pro targets German-speaking CNC machining shops (Lohnfertiger) with a SaaS solution for manufacturing time calculation and NC code generation. Initial focus: Saxony (home market), then expand to Bavaria and Baden-Württemberg.

**Target:** €9,000 ARR in 90 days (12 customers × €75/month intro price)

---

## Market Analysis

### Target Market Size (Germany)

| Segment | Count | TAM (€/year) |

| Attribute | Specification |
|-----------|---------------|
| Company size | 5-50 employees |
| Revenue | €500K - €10M |
| Location | Germany, Austria, Switzerland |
| Operations | CNC milling/turning, job shop |
| Pain point | Manual calculation, Excel chaos |

### Competitive Landscape

| Competitor | Strength | Weakness | Price |
|------------|----------|----------|-------|
| Excel (DIY) | Free, familiar | Error-prone, no NC code | €0 |
| CAM Systems | NC code, simulation | Expensive, complex | €500+/mo |

**Positioning:** "The fast, simple alternative between Excel and expensive CAM"

## Pricing Strategy

### Pricing Tiers

| Tier | Price | Target Customer |
|------|-------|-----------------|
| **Free Trial** | €0/30 days | Everyone |
| **Pro** | €99/month | Small shops (1-2 users) |
| **Business** | €149/month | Growing shops (multi-user) |
| **Enterprise** | Custom | Large manufacturers |

### Launch Pricing

**Intro offer (first 50 customers):**
- 50% off for first 3 months
- Pro: €49/month → €99/month after 3 months
- Business: €75/month → €149/month after 3 months

### Pricing Psychology

- Anchor on time savings (€85/hour × 10 hours = €850/month value)
- Compare to CAM software (€500+/month)
- Monthly billing, annual discount (2 months free)

## Sales Process

### Funnel Stages

```
Lead → Demo Request → Demo Call → Trial → Paid Customer
100%      20%            50%        40%      30%
```

### Lead Sources

| Source | % of Pipeline | Cost/Lead |
|--------|---------------|-----------|
| Cold outreach | 50% | Time only |
| Referrals | 20% | €0 |
| Organic (SEO/Content) | 20% | Time only |
| Paid ads | 10% | €20-50 |

### Sales Cycle

- **Average:** 2-3 weeks
- **Fastest:** Same day (demo → signup)
- **Longest:** 6 weeks (enterprise)

### Objection Handling

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "Too expensive" | "One saved hour pays for a month" |
| "We use Excel" | "Excel works. This is 10x faster and catches errors." |
| "Need to think" | "Try it free for 30 days. No risk." |
| "Our parts are complex" | "Let's do a live calculation with your part." |
