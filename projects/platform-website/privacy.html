Privacy Policy

Effective: [Launch Date]

At Ainary, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

1. What We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Email address, name, and password when you create an account
  • Payment Information: Billing details processed securely through Stripe (we do not store full credit card numbers)
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our services, including queries submitted, reports generated, and features accessed
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and cookies for analytics and functionality

2. How We Use It

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our intelligence tools and services
  • Process payments and manage your account
  • Communicate with you about updates, features, and support
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our AI models and platform
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents

3. What We Don't Do

We commit to the following:

  • We don't sell your data. Ever. Your information is not a product.
  • We don't train AI models on your queries. Your searches and reports remain private.
  • We don't share with third parties except for essential service providers (payment processing, infrastructure) who are bound by strict confidentiality agreements.

4. Cookies

We use cookies for essential functionality and privacy-respecting analytics (via Plausible or Umami, not Google Analytics). We do not use tracking cookies or sell cookie data to advertisers. You can disable cookies in your browser, but some features may not function properly.

5. Data Storage

Your data is encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest. We store data on secure, US-based servers. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction of your information.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data at any time
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data (we will retain some data as required by law)
  • Export: Download your reports and data in portable formats
  • Correction: Update or correct your account information
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails (service-related emails will still be sent)

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@ainary.com. We comply with GDPR for users in the European Union.

7. Third Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Stripe: Payment processing (subject to Stripe's Privacy Policy)
  • Plausible or Umami: Privacy-friendly analytics (no personal data tracked)
  • AI Providers: OpenAI, Anthropic, or similar for report generation (queries are anonymized and not stored by providers)

8. Data Retention

We retain your data as follows:

  • Account data: Until you delete your account
  • Reports: According to your subscription plan (e.g., 90 days for Starter, unlimited for Pro)
  • Payment records: As required by law for tax and accounting purposes (typically 7 years)
  • Usage logs: Up to 12 months for security and service improvement

9. Children

Our services are not intended for users under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or through our platform. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, contact us at privacy@ainary.com.
