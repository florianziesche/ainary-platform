Questions

What data sources do you use? +
We scan ArXiv papers, Hacker News, Reddit, GitHub, SEC filings, and curated industry feeds. Every claim is linked to its source. When data is missing, we say so.
How accurate are your reports? +
Every insight comes with a confidence indicator (High/Medium/Low). We're transparent about what we know and where data is missing. We'd rather tell you we don't know than guess.
How long does a report take? +
All reports complete in under 5 minutes. Five agents work in parallel — researching, analyzing, critiquing, and synthesizing — while you wait.
What's the difference between the three products? +
AI Readiness analyzes established companies — financial health, competitive position, AI adoption potential. Due Diligence is built for startups and investment decisions — confidence scoring, hidden signals, data gap disclosure. AI Research answers strategic questions with full evidence transparency — every claim labeled as Cited, Assumption, or Interpretation.
Can I analyze private companies? +
Yes. Our agents scan publicly available information — news, job postings, GitHub activity, patent filings, industry mentions. For private companies, confidence levels may be lower in financial sections — and we tell you that explicitly.
How is this different from ChatGPT? +
Three differences: (1) Five specialized agents instead of one general model. (2) Every claim is source-grounded with citations. (3) Three rounds of synthesis and critique before delivery. A single model gives you a summary. We give you analysis.
What's Hyperthink? +
Our 3-round process. Round 1: Research and analysis. Round 2: A Critic agent challenges every finding. Round 3: Final synthesis incorporating the critique. This catches errors and surfaces insights that single-pass AI misses.
Does the system improve over time? +
Yes. Your 10th report is smarter than your 1st. The system learns your industry focus and detects cross-report patterns — trends across your portfolio you wouldn't spot in isolation. Every analysis makes the next one better.
Is my data private? +
Yes. We don't store your queries or reports beyond your session. We don't use your data to train models. Your history is yours, always exportable.
