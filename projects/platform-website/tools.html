Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our intelligence tools.

What data sources do you use? +
We scan 243 sources including SEC filings, annual reports, ArXiv papers, news wires, industry reports, patent databases, GitHub, Reddit, Hacker News, and 35 specialized RSS feeds. Every claim in our reports is linked to its source.
How accurate are your reports? +
Every insight comes with a confidence indicator (High/Medium/Low). We're transparent about what we know, what we're uncertain about, and where data is missing. We'd rather tell you we don't know than guess.
How long does a report take? +
Corporate X-Ray reports typically complete in 2-3 minutes. Startup X-Ray reports take 3-5 minutes due to additional signal detection. The Daily Brief is generated automatically each morning.
What's the difference between Corporate X-Ray and Startup X-Ray? +
Corporate X-Ray is designed for established companies with public data (financials, filings, market position). Startup X-Ray is built for early-stage companies where data is scarce — it uses confidence indicators to show exactly where information gaps exist.
Can I analyze private companies? +
Yes. Our agents scan publicly available information including news, job postings, GitHub activity, patent filings, and industry mentions. For private companies, confidence levels may be lower in financial sections — and we'll tell you that explicitly.
How is this different from ChatGPT or Perplexity? +
Three key differences: (1) We use 5 specialized agents instead of one general model — each with a distinct role. (2) Every claim is source-grounded with citations. (3) Our Hyperthink process runs 3 rounds of synthesis and critique before delivering results.
What does 'Hyperthink' mean? +
Hyperthink is our 3-round synthesis process. Round 1: Initial research and analysis. Round 2: Critical review — a dedicated Critic agent challenges every finding. Round 3: Final synthesis incorporating the critique. This catches errors and surface insights that single-pass AI misses.
Is my data private? +
Yes. We don't store your queries or reports beyond your session. We don't use your data to train models. Enterprise plans include custom data isolation and SSO.
Can I export reports? +
Free plans include HTML reports. Pro and above include PDF export with your branding. Enterprise plans support white-label reports and API access.
What's the Daily Intelligence Brief? +
Every morning at 7 AM, our Research Engine scans 243 sources and delivers the signals that matter across four categories: AI & Tech, Enterprise, Startups & VC, and Tools & Products. Each signal includes our analysis, trend direction, and relevance score.
How is a report structured? +
A Corporate X-Ray report contains 13 sections across ~47 pages: Executive Summary, Company Overview, Financial Health Analysis, Competitive Landscape, Market Positioning, Strategic Risks, AI Readiness Assessment, Innovation Pipeline, Leadership Analysis, Regulatory Environment, Growth Opportunities, Implementation Roadmap, and Sources & Methodology. Each section includes confidence indicators, source citations, and actionable recommendations. Startup X-Ray reports follow a similar structure but add Confidence Assessment, Hidden Signals, and Investment Thesis sections.
