Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch between plans?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect immediately, with prorated billing adjustments.

What happens if I exceed my monthly limit?

You'll receive a notification when approaching your limit. You can either upgrade your plan or wait until the next billing cycle. No overage charges.

Is there a free trial for Pro or Enterprise?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for Pro. Enterprise trials are customized based on your organization's needs.

How does annual billing work?

Annual plans are billed upfront with a 20% discount. You'll save significantly compared to monthly billing while ensuring uninterrupted access.

What's included in a Custom Project?

Custom Projects are fully tailored to your organization. We build dedicated knowledge bases, train custom advisors on your data, and provide white-glove onboarding. Starting at €5,000, pricing depends on scope.
