## Executive Summary

This research identifies **high-impact, low-competition keywords** and SEO strategies to drive qualified traffic to Ainary's AI intelligence platform. Key findings:

- **Primary opportunity:** "AI company analysis" and "VC due diligence automation" have high search intent with moderate competition
- **Trending terms:** "Agentic AI," "multi-agent," and "AI agents" are dominating 2026 discourse (Gartner predicts 40% of enterprise apps will embed AI agents by 2026)
- **Competitor weakness:** Most competitors focus on broad "market intelligence" rather than specific AI-powered use cases
- **Quick wins:** Long-tail keywords like "startup analysis tool for VCs" and "AI due diligence platform" show lower competition
