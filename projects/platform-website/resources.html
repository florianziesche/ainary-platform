▶ Research Synthesis
Research Claude 
Analyze [topic]. Find 10+ credible sources. For each claim, assign a confidence level (High/Medium/Low) based on source quality and cross-verification. Present findings as: Claim → Evidence → Confidence → Source. Flag contradictions explicitly.
▶ Due Diligence Brief
VC Any Model 
Create a due diligence brief for [company]. Cover: Team (backgrounds, previous exits), Market (TAM/SAM/SOM with sources), Product (moat, defensibility), Traction (revenue, growth rate, retention), Competition (landscape map), Unit Economics (CAC, LTV, margins), Red flags. Use public sources only. Cite everything. End with: Invest / Pass / Watch + reasoning.
