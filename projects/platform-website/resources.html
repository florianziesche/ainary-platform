Reading List

Papers & Books That Shaped My Thinking

"Attention Is All You Need"

Vaswani et al., 2017

The transformer paper. Everything since — GPT, BERT, Claude — builds on this architecture.

"Scaling Laws for Neural Language Models"

Kaplan et al., 2020

Proved that model performance scales predictably with compute, data, and parameters. Changed how labs invest.

"Constitutional AI"

Anthropic, 2022

How to align AI systems using principles instead of human labels. The foundation of safe, useful models.

"The Bitter Lesson"

Rich Sutton, 2019

General methods that leverage computation always win. The most important essay in AI history.

"Situational Awareness"

Leopold Aschenbrenner, 2024

The most serious attempt to map where AI is going by 2027. Required reading for anyone in the space.

"Zero to One"

Peter Thiel

Contrarian thinking about building monopolies. Still the best book on startup strategy.

"The Hard Thing About Hard Things"

Ben Horowitz

No theory — just what it actually feels like to run a company when everything is breaking.
