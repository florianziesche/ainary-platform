Questions

How accurate are the reports?+
Every insight has a confidence indicator. I tell you what I know, what I'm guessing, and where data is missing. I'd rather say "I don't know" than guess.
How is this different from ChatGPT?+
Five specialized agents instead of one general model. Every claim source-grounded. Three rounds of synthesis and critique. A single model gives a summary. This gives analysis.
Can I analyze private companies?+
Yes. I scan public data — news, job postings, GitHub, patents, industry mentions. For private companies, confidence levels adjust automatically.
Does it improve over time?+
Yes. Your 10th report is smarter than your 1st. The system learns your focus and detects cross-report patterns you wouldn't spot alone.

Yes. No storage beyond your session. No model training on your data. Always exportable.
