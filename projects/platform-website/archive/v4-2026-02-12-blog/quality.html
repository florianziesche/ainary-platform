HOW WE BUILD

Quality isn't a feature. It's architecture.

Most AI tools are a single prompt wrapped in a UI. We built something fundamentally different: a multi-agent system that researches, critiques, and synthesizes — the way the best consulting teams work.

Human × AI = Compounded Intelligence

Fewer agents. Deeper analysis.

We didn't add agents until we ran out of ideas. Each one has a specific job. They work in parallel, challenge each other, then synthesize. More agents would dilute the process. Five is the sweet spot.

Researcher
Scans 243 sources. Finds what matters.
Analyst
Structures raw data into insights.
Strategist
Connects dots. Identifies patterns.
Critic
Challenges every conclusion.
Synthesizer
Distills into actionable intelligence.

Every insight survives three rounds of scrutiny.

1
Generate
5 agents work independently. No groupthink.
2
Critique
Each agent challenges the others' findings. Weak claims get eliminated.
3
Synthesize
Surviving insights merge into a coherent intelligence report. Cited sources included.

We tell you what we don't know.

Most AI tools hallucinate with confidence. We built confidence indicators into every insight. Green means strong data. Yellow means limited sources. Red means we're speculating — and we'll tell you why.

High Confidence
