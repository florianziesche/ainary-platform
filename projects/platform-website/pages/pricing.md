# Pricing Page — Do/Don'ts & Spec

## Ziel
User versteht in 10 Sekunden: Credits-System, was er bekommt, welcher Plan passt.
Primary Action: "Start Pro Trial" oder "Get Started Free"

## Modell: Credits (Lovable-Style)
- 1 Credit = 1 X-Ray Report OR 1 Advisory Board Session OR 30 Daily Brief Days

## Struktur

### 1. Nav (identisch zu Landing)

### 2. Header
- Headline: "Pricing" (Geist 2.5rem)
- Subtitle: "Start free. Scale as you grow." (Inter 300, --text-secondary)
- Toggle: Monthly / Annual (20% save)

### 3. Pricing Cards (4 Spalten, Linear-Style 1px Gap Grid)
- Free ($0): "For individuals exploring intelligence tools."
  - 3 credits/month, Basic reports, Community support
  - CTA: "Get Started" (Outline)
- Pro ($29/mo): "For professionals making strategic decisions." — FEATURED
  - 50 credits/month, PDF export, Research-backed advisors, Priority support
  - CTA: "Start Pro Trial" (Gold Primary)
  - Badge: "MOST POPULAR"
- Business ($99/mo): "For teams requiring depth and scale."
  - 200 credits/month, API access, White-label reports, Team features
  - CTA: "Contact Sales" (Outline)
- Custom (€5,000+): "For organizations with dedicated needs."
  - Unlimited credits, Custom integrations, SLA, Dedicated support
  - CTA: "Schedule Call" (Outline)

### 4. How Credits Work
- 3 Items: "1 Credit = 1 X-Ray" / "1 Credit = 1 Advisory Session" / "1 Credit = 30 Daily Brief Days"
- Clean, simple, JetBrains Mono for "1 Credit"

### 5. Feature Comparison Table (unter den Cards, wie Linear)
- Kategorien: Intelligence Tools, Research, Export, Support, Team
- Rows mit Check/X Icons pro Tier
- Scrollable auf Mobile

### 6. FAQ (5-6 Fragen)
- Accordion-style (click to expand)

### 7. Bottom CTA + Footer

## Do
- Tier descriptions: EINE Zeile, kursiv oder --text-secondary
- Preise groß in Geist Semibold
- "/month" klein in --text-muted
- Feature-Liste mit Check-Icons (Lucide, --accent)
- Annual Toggle mit "Save 20%" Badge
- Pro Card: --accent-border, subtle gold gradient bg

## Don't
- KEINE langen Feature-Listen in den Cards (max 4-5 Items)
- Detaillierter Vergleich in separater Tabelle darunter
- KEINE Emoji
- KEIN Glassmorphism
- NICHT "cheap" oder "affordable" — "Start free" reicht
