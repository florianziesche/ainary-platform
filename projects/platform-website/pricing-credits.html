Frequently Asked Questions

Do credits expire?

No. Credits never expire. They roll over month to month, so you can save them for when you need them most.

Can I buy additional credits?

Yes. You can purchase additional credit packs at any time. Top-ups are available in 10, 25, or 50-credit bundles.

What happens if I run out of credits?

You'll receive a notification when you're running low. You can either upgrade your plan or purchase additional credits. Service continues normally.

Can I switch between plans?

Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Credits carry over, and billing adjusts automatically.

Is there a free trial?

Free tier requires no credit card and includes 3 credits to get started. Pro and Business plans include a 14-day trial with full access.

What's included in Custom plans?

Custom plans are built for your organization's needs. We provide dedicated infrastructure, custom integrations, white-glove onboarding, and SLAs. Starting at €5,000 per project.
