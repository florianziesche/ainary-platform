**Text (optional, für Show HN):**
I spent 10+ years building AI products (computer vision SaaS, raised €5.5M). The one thing I keep seeing: every agent framework helps you build agents, none help you trust them.

LLMs are overconfident 84% of the time. When your agent says "95% confident," that number means nothing.

AgentTrust adds three primitives to any agent framework:
- Calibration (ask 3x independently, measure agreement)
- Trust scoring (mandatory metadata on every output)  
- Confidence-aware routing (high confidence → auto-execute, low → human review)

We also published our first research report: "State of AI Agent Trust 2026" — 24 sources, multi-agent synthesis, everything open source including methodology.

Report: https://ainaryventures.com/article-agenttrust.html
GitHub: https://github.com/fziescheus-alt/agenttrust

Built as a solo founder with AI agents doing 80% of the research. Happy to answer questions about the approach.
