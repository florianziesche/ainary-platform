## The List

### Tier 1: VC Perspectives (How investors think)

**1. Sequoia Capital Blog**
- URL: https://www.sequoiacap.com/blog/

- Best for: Founder advice, market perspectives, pattern recognition

**2. Y Combinator Blog**
- URL: https://www.ycombinator.com/blog/
- Why: Early-stage insights from the source
- Best for: Startup tactics, founder stories, what's getting funded

**3. Greylock Partners Blog**
- URL: https://greylock.com/blog/

## How I Use These

### Weekly Routine:
1. **Blogwatcher** (or your RSS reader) pulls all new posts
2. **AI scans** and extracts key insights
3. **I read the digest** (~15 minutes)
4. **Deep dive** on 1-2 articles that matter most
5. **Capture** insights to Obsidian

### What to Look For:
- Contrarian takes (disagreement = learning opportunity)
- Data points worth remembering
- Frameworks I can apply
- Names/companies to track

### What to Skip:
- Promotional content
- News without analysis
- Anything you've seen 3x already

## Setup Instructions

1. Use Feedly, Inoreader, or NetNewsWire
2. Add these 10 feeds
3. Check 2-3x per week
4. Star what matters, archive the rest

### Option B: AI-Powered (My Setup)
1. Use Blogwatcher CLI + OpenClaw
2. Feeds auto-sync daily
3. AI extracts insights
4. Weekly digest generated automatically

### Option C: Email-Based
Most of these offer email newsletters:
- Subscribe to email versions
- Create a "Reading" folder/label
