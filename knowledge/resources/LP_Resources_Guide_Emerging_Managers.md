# LP Resources & Networking Guide for Emerging [[VC]] Managers
*Research compiled: February 7, 2026*

---

## üéØ Executive Summary

This guide provides actionable resources for emerging [[VC]] managers (Fund I-III) to discover LPs, build networks, and automate deal flow monitoring. Focus areas: LP databases, emerging manager accelerators, NYC networking, and automated monitoring systems.

**Key Insight:** For emerging managers, family offices often close faster than institutional LPs. Most successful Fund I/II raises come from 60-120 highly targeted LPs, not mass outreach to thousands.

### **Tier 1: Purpose-Built for Emerging Managers**

#### **Altss** ‚≠ê (Recommended for Fund I-III)
- **Focus:** OSINT-powered family office & LP intelligence specifically for fundraisers
- **Coverage:** 
  - 9,000+ verified family offices (North America, Europe, Middle East, Asia)
  - 1.5M+ LP contacts across family offices, institutions, angels, wealth platforms
- **Pricing:**
  - **Emerging Manager Tier (Fund I-II):**

- **Strengths:**
  - 30-day refresh cadence (live data vs. stale contacts)
  - Thesis-based search (sector, geography, ticket size, thematic interest)
  - Enrichment for personalized outreach (recent investments, focus areas)
  - Custom research service for niche LP lists
- **Coming Soon:**
  - **Full LP Coverage** (Feb 2026): Expands to endowments, pensions, sovereigns, FoFs

- **Reviews:** SourceForge & G2 reviewers highlight freshness, low bounce rates, detailed profiles
- **Best For:** Emerging managers focused on family offices and niche LP targeting

#### **Dakota Marketplace**
- **Focus:** U.S.-based institutional investors; sales enablement approach
- **Coverage:** 14,000+ DB, DC, Taft-Hartley plans; strong on institutional allocators
- **Pricing:** ~$5,000-$25,000/year (depends on seats, usage)
- **Strengths:**
  - Deep institutional investor data

  - Limited family office coverage

### **Tier 2: Institutional Research Tools (Complement, Not Primary)**

#### **Preqin**
- **Focus:** Institutional allocator mandates at scale (pensions, endowments, sovereigns)
- **Pricing:** $15,000-$50,000/year (module-dependent)
- **Strengths:**
  - Comprehensive historical fund performance data
  - Strong for benchmarking and macro LP analysis
- **Weaknesses:**
  - Contact data can lag real job moves
  - Not optimized for "show me 60 LPs likely to back my Fund I this year"
  - Heavy platform; assumes larger IR teams
- **Website:** https://www.preqin.com
- **Best For:** Multi-hundred-million raises, institutional research, benchmarking

#### **PitchBook**
- **Focus:** Deal analytics, M&A tracking, co-investment mapping
- **Pricing:** $30,000+/year
- **Strengths:**
  - Industry standard for deal intelligence
  - Excellent for valuations, exit dynamics, fund benchmarking
- **Weaknesses:**
  - LP profiles more historical than dynamic
  - Not tuned for day-to-day fundraising outreach
- **Website:** https://pitchbook.com
- **Best For:** Deal research, competitive analysis; pair with a dedicated LP tool

#### **FINTRX**
- **Focus:** Private wealth mapping (family offices, RIAs, advisors)
- **Coverage:** 4,400+ single/multi-family offices globally
- **Pricing:** ~$15,000/year (confirm with vendor)
- **Strengths:**
  - Detailed org charts for wealth firms
  - Broad RIA and advisor coverage
- **Weaknesses:**
  - Platform can feel complex initially
  - Data accuracy varies (check G2 reviews for balanced perspective)
- **Website:** https://www.fintrx.com
- **Reviews:** https://www.g2.com/products/fintrx
- **Best For:** Mapping family office structures and intermediary networks

### **Stack Recommendation for Emerging Managers**

**Budget-Conscious (Fund I, <$10M raise):**
- **Core:** Altss (Family Office module) - $10,000/year
- **Free/Low-Cost:** RAISE Emerging Manager Database (see below), Visible.vc Connect, manual research

**Well-Funded (Fund II/III, $20M+ raise):**
- **Core LP Discovery:** Altss (Full LP coverage) - $12,000/year
- **Research Layer:** Preqin or PitchBook (~$15-30K) for benchmarking
- **Institutional Add-On:** Dakota Marketplace if targeting U.S. pensions/endowments

**Insight:** Many teams use Preqin/PitchBook for macro research and Altss as the daily LP pipeline engine.
